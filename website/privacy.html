Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026 · Placeholder draft pending legal review

1. Who we are

Tigers One Exchange (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a licensed currency and digital-asset exchange operating in the Kingdom of Thailand. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you use our services, visit our offices, or engage with us through any communication channel.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our service safely and to meet Thai regulatory standards. This may include your name, contact details, identification documents for transactions above regulatory thresholds, transaction history, and communications with our specialists.

We do not require account registration to use most of our services. Information is collected at the point of transaction, only as needed.

3. How we use your information

We process personal information to execute exchanges and settlements, comply with anti-money-laundering (AML) and counter-terrorist-financing (CTF) obligations, prevent fraud, respond to your enquiries, and improve our service. We never use your information for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of information

We share information only when legally required (with regulators, law enforcement under valid order, or our auditors), with banking partners necessary to settle a transaction, or with vetted service providers bound by confidentiality. Your information never leaves our jurisdictional framework without lawful basis.

5. Data retention

We retain transaction records for the period required by Thai law (currently a minimum of 10 years for AML compliance). Other personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose collected and is securely deleted thereafter.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention obligations. To exercise these rights, contact our compliance team at the address below. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use encrypted communications, access controls, ISO-aligned data-handling practices, and physical security at all office locations. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your information with the discretion expected of a private-client service.

8. Cookies & analytics

Our website uses essential cookies to remember your language preference and ensure functionality. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics may be collected to improve the service.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified through our website. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@tigersoneexchange.com.
